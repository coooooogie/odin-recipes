The "Red Pizza"

Description

The red pizza

A margherita, but we don't call it that... Known as red pizza in this household. You must always make 50% of your dough as red.

Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 250g Fermented Dough
  • Red Sauce
  • Mozzarella
  • Parmigiano
  • Fresh Basil
    •
    1. Stretch dough
    2. Top with sauce, mozzarella and parmigiano
    3. Cook in pizza oven until done
    4. Set on rack to cool and top with fresh basil leaves
    5. Cut and enjoy!
