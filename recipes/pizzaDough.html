Pizza Dough

Description

The perfect fermented pizza dough.

Ready in as little as 6 hours, but fermented up to 3 days in the fridge. The key to this recipe is developing the flavour of the dough through the pre-fermentation process.

Enjoy!

Ingredients

Makes 3 250g balls of dough

  • 500g - Tipo 00 Flour
  • 2.3g - Dry Active Yeast
  • 1g - Honey
  • 13g - Salt
  • 440g - Water
  • 23g - Olive Oil
    •

    The Pre-Ferment

    1. Warm water to 140 degrees farenheight
    2. Combine water and yeast, allow to bloom
    3. Mix flour until incorporated
    4. Autolyse for 15 minutes and mix once more to remove any lumps
    5. Let rest min 1 hour at room temperature or up to overnight in the fridge

    The Bulk Ferment

    1. Add oil, salt, and remaining 60g of flour
    2. Mix and allow to autolyse for 15 minutes
    3. Return to dough and mix until gluten and smooth surface is developed
    4. Let rest until doubled in size
    5. Return and divide in to 250g dough balls
    6. Let rest 3-6 hours depending on heat and humidity
